Privacy Policy — Tesbih

Last updated: 2026-04-27

The Tesbih Android app (package org.workshopdiy.tesbih) is developed by Abdelhak Bourdim under the workshop-diy.org banner. This policy describes what data the app collects — which is nothing.

Data we collect: none

The app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. There is no account system, no login, no email capture, no telemetry, no analytics, no crash reporting, no advertising network, and no third-party SDKs. All content is bundled inside the app and runs entirely on your device.

Permissions

The only permission requested is INTERNET, used solely to load external links if you explicitly tap them (e.g. the workshop-diy.org link in the header). The app makes no other network requests.

Storage

The app saves small preferences on your device's local storage — chosen language, chosen theme, quiz progress. This data never leaves your phone.

Children

The app is rated for Everyone and contains no content unsuitable for minors. It does not knowingly collect data from users of any age, since it does not collect data at all.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be published at this same URL with a new "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after any change means you accept the updated policy.
